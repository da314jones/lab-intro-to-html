General Tso's Chicken

Description

General Tso's Chicken is saucy, juicy and crispy. The signature sauce is thick and loaded with flavor. If you are a die-hard orange chicken fan, you must try this dish! Make it all in one dish in the comfort of your home for a sweet and spicy treat!

Ingredients

Marinade for the Chicken
  • 1 lb. Boneless & skinless chicken breast, cut into 1-inch cubes.
  • 1 tsp. Baking soda
  • 1/4. tsp Black pepper
  • 2 tsp. Shaoxing wine
For the Sauce
  • 2 Tbs. Sugar
  • 1 Tbs. Sambal
  • 1 Tbs. Soy sauce
  • 3 Tbs. White vinegar
  • 2 Tbs. Shaoxing wine
  • 1/4 cup Chicken stock
  • 1 tsp. Corn starch
For the Batter
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup Corn starch
  • Sesame seeds
For Frying & Stir-Fry
  • Frying online
  • 2 tsp. Chili oil with chili flakes
  • 5 to 6 dried chili
  • 4 Cloves garlic, roughly chopped

Directions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine all Ingredients of marinade for chicken; mix well with your hand and set aside.
  2. In another mixing bowl, combine all Ingredients os sauce; whisk until sugar has dissolved. Set aside
  3. Pour frying oil into your wok, at least 1-inch deep. (adjust for your size wok.) Heat oil over medium/high heat to 325degrees
  4. While oil is heating up, add egg and corn starch into marinated chicken to create batter. Mix with your hands very well.
  5. Add half of chicken to wok one by one (Carefully as no to splash oil). Make sure the chicken doesn't stick to each other and not crowd the pot. (Do this in several small batches) Fry for 2-3 min and turn over, fry for 2 additional min or till golden brown. Stir with as needed. Remove from wok place in plate or bowl lined with paper towel. Repeat until all chicken is done. Place to the side.
  6. Let chicken cool completely before second round of deep fry. This process will make the chicken super crunchy. Increase heat to 375 degrees, deep fry the chicken about 1 min. Remove and place on plate or bowl with fresh paper towels. Repeat with remainder of chicken till done.
  7. Discard or remove (for some other use), the oil from wok.
  8. Lightly hand wipe wok with paper towel inside and out. Add dried chili, chili flakes, 2tsp. chili oil and chopped garlic into cold wok. Raise heat to medium/high. Stir with spatula till fragrance is present (careful not to burn garlic), 30 sec to 1 min.
  9. Add chicken and sauce then toss together and allow chicken to be evenly coated with sauce, about 30 - 40 sec in, remove from heat immediately.
  10. Transfer to serving dish. If desired sprinkle with sesame seeds. Serve with choice of rice or noodle!
